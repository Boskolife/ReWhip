Frequently Asked Questions

Who Are We?

We’re car buyers and tech enthusiasts who’ve faced the same challenges in searching for the perfect car. That’s why we created ReWhip — to simplify the process and make it faster and more intuitive.

Why ReWhip?

ReWhip combines listings from multiple marketplaces, offering a more extensive selection of cars. Instead of browsing multiple sites, you can find everything in one place.

Are the cars up to date?

Yes, all cars listed are currently active in the market. Listings are continuously updated to ensure you’re viewing the latest available cars. If a car is sold or removed, it’s promptly updated on the platform.

How does AI enhance my search?

Tell us what you're looking for, and our AI does the rest. It optimizes your search by narrowing down options based on your preferences, ensuring you get the most relevant results.

Can I save my favorite cars?

Yes, you can easily save cars you like for quick access later. This feature helps you compare options and keep track of potential buys.

Can I contact sellers directly?

Yes, all sellers’ contact details are available on ReWhip, and you can reach out to them directly for more information or to arrange a test drive.

Do I need an account to use ReWhip?

You don’t need an account to browse, but creating one lets you save your favorite cars for quick access, to manage subscriptions and set alerts for new market arrivals. It’s quick and easy to sign up.
